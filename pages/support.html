Office Setup and Support

From setting up a new office or revamping an existing one. We will run all the background tasks to get your business running smoothly. We believe in matching IT systems with your business requirements with the opportunity to extend as your business expands. Everything is offered as a fixed rate so there are no hidden costs or overheads.

Once your office is set up and running we continue to monitor your service remotely and can trouble shoot problems early. Where necessary we provide technical support to ensure running of your business is not disrupted.

Support Desk

We provide access to our help desk via email, phone and web – 7 days a week. This will be your first point of call for making requests to our team.

Remote Assistance

Majority of problems can be fixed by remote support. We’ll connect to you and resolve the query or problem remotely saving time and money. Remote Assistance can be provided on a case by case basis or we can deploy our remote agent to your computers for faster ticket resolution. If your computer has access to the internet we can usually resolve it remotely.

Onsite Support

If the issue can’t be resolved remotely, we’ll come out to your office and get you up and running. Should we find a fault with your eqlipment, we are able to repair the most common failures on site. If it’s beyond repair we’ll advise the best steps to take. In the mean time, we have loan equipment available for your use so you can keep on working.
