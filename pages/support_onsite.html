Onsite Support


There are times where you just cant fix things remotely. Maybe the internet connection is broken or a centralised hub has stopped working. No worries, we will send out a technician to get you working.

You can build in onsite support as part of managed services package. Alternately you can use helping hands solution as a temporary measure.

If you computers are unserviceable we will look at alternate solution that include using our servers on the Cloud to get you back on track.

Whatever the option you choose we will get you up and running in no time.
